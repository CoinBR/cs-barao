A Team Based Game

Teamwork makes CS Legendary

Rejecting the individual nature of traditional First Personal Shooters like Doom, Quake and Unreal, Counter-Strike pits teams against each other in highly-intense matches where every single bullet counts. While in Quake deathmatch was predominant, in Counter-Strike strategy takes the cake. Still, your individual skills can play a big hole as long as you remember you’re part of a team, just look at some amazing kills professionals players posts on youtube and you will have an idea. Like StarCraft, where other RTS games had come before it, other tactical shooters had come before CS. But the perfect elements of game play and physics melded together and created a dedicated fan base which took the game to its legendary status. The fast pace and high-pressure decision making processes are combined with a level of strategy and teamwork that are even more intense because of the high level teams we can see in each tournament. Teamwork makes CS legendary and gives it an appeal that only legendary games can attain.
