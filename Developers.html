Creative Community

"E os CTs (terceiros) vão descendo a ladeira...

Levando tiro pela frente pelas costas..."

Counter-Strike itself is a MOD. It have encouraged players to continue this creative process creating new maps, models, sounds and all kind of features.

One good example is cs_rio. A big map, fully developed by the Brazilians “Mataleone” and “Crocodilo”. The map brings the game to a Brazilian slum in the city of “Rio de Janeiro”. In this map, the CTs represents the Brazilian police, trying to invade the slum and rescue the hostages held by Brazilians drug dealers, represented by the Terrorists. It features a lot of typical Brazilian environments such a small bar and a soccer field.

cs_rio have awarded the QBranch competition as the best layout, atmosphere and textures. QBranch also pointed “Mataleone”, one of the creators of the map, as the best mapper, and mapper revelation.
