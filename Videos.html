Frag Movies

Counter-Strike is a very competitive game who does focuses only in gameplay but also reward players for his individual skills. This competitiveness engage a lot of players to record his best moves and join them together in what is called a “Frag Movie”. We can see professional players doing all kind of unbelievable things in these Frag Movies. This inspires the amateur players to become better and better in the game, and also to publish their own kills. Most of Counter-Strike players have been watching Frag Movies for years, getting inspired seeing the amazing skills, strategies of professional and amateur players. In these movies we can also see players winning matches in tournament and receiving prizes while the crowd cheers! All Counter-Strike players has the same dream: One day be good enough to live that experience.
