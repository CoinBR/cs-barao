What is Counter-Strike?

the Mod who Ruled the World

Counter-Strike was originally a MOD of Half Life made by Minh “Gooseman” Le and Jess “Cliffe” Cliffe (Wikipedia). A tactical shooter that became an instant online success. It would later be released in 2000 by Valve, the original producers of Half- Life. Most First-Person Shooters before Counter-Strike (CS) such as Quake and Unreal Tournament were futuristic and crazily fast. So Counter-Strike and change all of this. All the FPS that came after Counter-Strike were highly influenced by this new style. Counter-Strike surpassed the popularity of remarkable games such as Quake III and Unreal Tournament. It is the most recognizable game of all the times.

Even today, CS stills the most widely-played PC shooter in the world.
