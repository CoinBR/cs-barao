Missions

The bomb has been planted

Counter-Strike’s features two opposing teams, Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists, fighting against each other in three official game types:

Hostage Rescue

Counter-Terrorists must rescue AI characters from a terrorist stronghold.

Bomb Defusal

Terrorists tries to plant bombs at specific sites while counter-terrorists attempt to defuse them before they explode.

Assassination

One counter-terrorist is randomly assigned as the “VIP” and must be escorted to the escape zone before terrorists can kill him.
