A Brilliant Counter-Terrorist vs Terrorist Execution

Counter-Terrorists, commonly abbreviated as CTs, are one of the two forces featured in the Counter-Strike series. They are often perceived as the "good guys", opposite of the bad guys, the Terrorists. Their traits is having a slight tactical advantage on some maps. However, many of their weapons are expensive.

Terrorists, commonly referred to as Ts (or TR in Brazil) , are the other force featured in all of the Counter-Strike games. They are often perceived as the "bad guys". They have a cheaper, still strong, armament.
